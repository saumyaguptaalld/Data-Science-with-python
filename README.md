## Approach

The workflow includes data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualization to uncover patterns in campaign performance. Metrics such as engagement, conversions, and cost efficiency are examined to evaluate effectiveness.

## Key Findings

- Campaign 1 achieved a significantly higher conversion rate (44.93%) compared to Campaign 2 (1.56%) and Campaign 3 (2.43%)
- High-engagement users (Level 3) contributed the majority of conversions (>600,000), while low-engagement users had minimal impact
- Weekly sales peaked at $3.13M in mid-May, followed by a steady decline, with June clicks falling to less than 50% of April levels
- Ad cost and revenue show a strong positive correlation (0.76), whereas engagement has a weak correlation with revenue (0.18)
- Placement “ghi” recorded the highest CTR (2.09%), and select banner sizes (728x90, 240x400) in Campaign 3 delivered the highest ROAS (1054)
